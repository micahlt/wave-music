Link a Spotify Account (Beta)

By linking a Spotify account, you gain access to your entire Spotify library, without the need to upload.
Songs, Albums, and Playlists you've saved on Spotify will appear among your uploaded songs.

Upload Music

Uploading your Music to Wave adds it to your Songs list. This also allows you to add it to local playlists.
The song will be saved on your device so it functions, even when offline. To remove a song, simply locate the song in the songs list, tap the more button, and tap delete.

Theme

Wave music has three theme options. The themes are light (default), dark, and black. You can set a theme, or automatically set it based on the time of day.
